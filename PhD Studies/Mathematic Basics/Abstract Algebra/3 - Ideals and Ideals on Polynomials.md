## Ideals$

>[!def] Definition Ideal on Rings ([[../../../PDFs/judson2022.pdf#page=208|Source]])
>Let $R$ be a [[1 - Algebraic Structures#^2c4055|ring]] and $I\subset R$ a subring. We say **<span style="color:#38ffa9">$I$ is an ideal</span>** if
>$$\forall a \in I, \forall r \in R: \;\; ar  \in I \land ra \in I$$
>or similiarly
>$$\forall r \in R: \;\;rI \subset I \land Ir \subset I$$
>We **<span style="color:#38ffa9">denote the ideal generated by $a \in R$</span>** with
>$$\langle a \rangle = \{ ar \mid r \in R \} \subset R$$ 
>>[!example] Example: $\{ 0 \}$ and $R$ are the <u>trivial ideals</u> with respect to $R$

>[!def] Definition Principal Ideal ([[../../../PDFs/judson2022.pdf#page=208|Source]])
>  Let $R$ be a [[1 - Algebraic Structures#^4a5286|commutative ring]] with identity, then the ideal
>  $$\langle a \rangle = \{ ar \mid r \in R \} \subset R$$
>  is called **<span style="color:#38ffa9">principal ideal</span>**.

>[!theorem] Theorem Principal Ideals of $\mathbb{Z}$ ([[../../../PDFs/judson2022.pdf#page=208|Source]])
>Every ideal in the ring of integer $\mathbb{Z}$ is a principal ideal.
>>[!proof]
>>

## Ideals in $F[x]$

>[!def] Definition Principal Ideal in $F[x]$ ([[../../../PDFs/judson2022.pdf#page=230|Source]])
>Let $F$ be a [[1 - Algebraic Structures#^a17f1c|field]]. $F[x]$ is an ideal $\langle p(x)\rangle$ generated by some polynomial $p(x)$, i.e.
>$$\langle p(x)\rangle= \{ p(x)q(x):q(x) \in F[x] \}$$
>
>>[!example] Example: The polynomial $x^2$ in $F[x]$ generates the ideal $\langle x^2 \rangle$

>[!theorem] Theorem  ([[../../../PDFs/judson2022.pdf#page=230|Source]])
>If $F$ is a field [[1 - Algebraic Structures#^a17f1c|field]], then every ideal in **<span style="color:#38ffa9">$F[x]$ is a principal ideal.</span>**

>[!theorem] Theorem ([[../../../PDFs/judson2022.pdf#page=230|Source]])
>Let $F$ be a [[1 - Algebraic Structures#^a17f1c|field]] and suppose that $p(x) \in F[x]$. **<span style="color:#38ffa9">Then the ideal generated by $p(x)$ is maximal if and only if $p(x)$ is irreducible.</span>**
